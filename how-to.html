Getting Started

Welcome! This guide will walk you through all the features of the application. The project loads with a sample family tree to demonstrate its capabilities. To start fresh, simply click the red trash can icon () at the bottom right. This will clear the canvas and present a large green plus button to add your first family member.

Navigating the Tree

Your family tree is an interactive canvas. You can move around it easily:

  • Pan: Click and drag on any empty space on the background to move the entire tree around.
  • Zoom: Use the +/- buttons in the bottom-right corner to zoom in and out. You can also use your mouse wheel for more precise control.
  • Reset View: Click the circular arrow button () to reset the zoom, position, and any active filters to the default state.
  • Fullscreen: Use the expand icon () to enter a distraction-free fullscreen mode.

Editing a Person

Each person on the tree is represented by a card. To edit someone's details:

  • Open the Editor: Simply double-click on any person's card, or hover over it and click the pencil icon ().
  • Update Information: In the pop-up window, you can change their name, assign a role from the dropdown, set birth/death dates, and add personal stories.
  • Change Photo: Click the camera icon on the profile picture to upload a new image from your device.
  • Add Tags: Use the tags field to add descriptive labels like "Military," "Artist," or "Immigrant."

Managing Relationships

The connections between people are managed in the Controls sidebar, providing a powerful, centralized way to build your tree.

  • Open Controls: Click the menu icon () in the header to open the sidebar.
  • Create a Link: In the "Relationship Manager" section, select two people from the "From" and "To" dropdowns, choose the relationship type, and click "Create Link."
  • Edit/Delete: Existing relationships are listed below the form. Use the pencil () and trash () icons to edit or remove any connection.

Using the Controls

The "Controls" button () in the header opens the sidebar with powerful tools:

  • Focus Search: The search bar in the main menu lets you quickly find and center the view on a specific person and their immediate family.
  • Filters: Use the "Family Side" and "Generation" dropdowns to show only specific parts of your family tree.
  • Toggle Lines: Use the "Show Lines" switch to hide or show all the connecting branches for a cleaner view.

Exporting & Saving

You can save and share your work in various formats from the "Export Options" dropdown in the Controls sidebar.

  • PNG/PDF/SVG: These options create a high-quality visual snapshot of your family tree, perfect for printing, sharing, or embedding elsewhere.
  • CSV: This option exports all the raw data (names, dates, notes) into a spreadsheet format, which is great for backups or data analysis.
